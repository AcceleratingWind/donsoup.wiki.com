Russian Translation

A Russian Translation of Soup 0.9 was made by AcceleratingWind on the 26th of November, 2023. It was updated on the 7th of January, 2024 with a couple of additional translated content.

List of translated content

• All 4 variations of the Title Screen, including the green Stereo ("Стерео") overlay;

• The Day Select;

• The small Movement pop up;

• The Oyasuminasai Screen;

• The text "POST" ("ПОЧТА") on the object from Town Room;

• The object from Telephone Room.

• The game's name on its window. (07.01.2024 Update)

• The fullscreen pop up message. (07.01.2024 Update)
