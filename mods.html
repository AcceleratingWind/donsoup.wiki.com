Mods

Various modifications of the original game, alongside fangames of it have been created since Soup 0.9's release.

Below is a list of all ones that I know of.

Sections

Mods

Mods are modified versions of the vanilla game, where creator(s) make their own version of the game with their own ideas.

• soupq and souqqqqqq, made by ijjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjj

• Rooms Of The Room Rooms, made by Agent-RedJackal

• VineRooms, made by Agent-RedJackal

• BOOPowo, made by Fishy Pug Bruh

• Soap Soup, led by Kite Line

• puos, made by... AcceleratingWind...

Translations

A sub-section of Mods, Translations merely translate the text contents of the game in a different language.

• Russian Translations, made by AcceleratingWind

Fangames

Fangames are games inspired by the original game, without directly modifying it.

• Lucid, made by FloofyNezzled

• Soft.Ware:0.2, led by AcceleratingWind

• absinthe, made by yoka

• SOUP (placeholder name), led by Kite Line

Recreations

Recreations simply recreate Soup 0.9 in different ways instead.

• Roblox recreation, made by kkurisu

• VR Chat recreations, made by Trixelized

• Unity recreation on Game Jolt, made by CruFro, it can be played in your browser

Modding Tutorials

Both hosma and Agent-RedJackal have made video tutorials about modding the game. These can be helpful if you want to make your own Soup 0.9 mod.
