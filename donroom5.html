Poem Room (donroom5) is a room accessible from Day 4. It was made by Yarhalla/Don.

Features

The room contains black walls with white japanese symbols, potentially hiding a poem of some sort.

This is one of the two rooms in the game to not have an object in the middle, first being Mouth Room.

This is also one of the two rooms that share the wind "song", with Corrupted Drawing Room from Day 5 using it later on.
