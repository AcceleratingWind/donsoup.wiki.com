Cubic Room (tarroom1) is a room accessible from Day 2. It was made by taro.

Features

The room features white walls and floor with sparkles on them, with the floor having a rainbow gradient.

The object seems to be that of a white cube-like structure, having similarities to a logo of Nintendo's console, the Gamecube, although it may be unintentional.
