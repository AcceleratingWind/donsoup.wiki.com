Title Screen

When you first start the game, you see a faucet with running water. You click, and water stops. Click one more time, and the water starts running again... except it is now ominously black.

Afterwards, one of the four different titles is shown, and you begin your first day of the game.

There is also a green overlay with a large number 10 and some japanese text underneath it.
