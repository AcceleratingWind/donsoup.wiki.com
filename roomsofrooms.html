Rooms of The Room Rooms

Rooms of The Room Rooms is a mod for Soup 0.9, made by Agent-Red Jackal. It was released in December of 2017.

The mod is centered around Tommy Wiseau's work, particularly the movie The Room, as well as memes associated with his work.

The game's file names haven't changed much, aside from there being a new music file "likehell.ogg". Days are named Rooms, with Day 5 being simply named Wiseau. Graphics for the unused Days 6 and 7 remain unchanged however.
