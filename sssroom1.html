Droplet Room (sssroom1) is a room accessible from Day 4. It was made by Sasa Sasaki.

Features

The room follows up Rainy Room from the previous day, with the rain going away and the music being multiple harmonic bells instead. The floor of Rainy Room is reused here.

The object is pretty abstract, seemingly a puddle with colorful droplets appearing and floating above it.
