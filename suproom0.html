Technology Room (suproom0) is a room accessible from Day 3. It was made by supply.

Features

The room features a wooden floor and walls, with the latter showing a tool similar to a compass, or a measuring device like the barometer or the ammeter.

The object is simply a black power plug, which is cut-off from it's wire. It's unknown who it belongs to.
