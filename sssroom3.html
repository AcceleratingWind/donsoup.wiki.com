Ocean Room (sssroom3) is a room accessible from Day 2. It was made by Sasa Sasaki.

Features

The room is seemingly set underwater, with dolphins being featured on the walls and floor. The music consists of a droning "choir".

The object seems to be a woman facing away from the player, similar to Day 1's Apartment and Sky Rooms.

The object's variant appears in Cyber Room from the same day.
