Days

Days are a very important part of Soup 0.9's gameplay.

As of the latest version of the game (0.91), there are a total of 5 Days, however, there may have been planned to be more.

Each Day has 5 Rooms the player can walk around in, which in total is 25 rooms in the whole game.

Completing a Day will either bring up the Oyasuminasai Screen or the Day Select based on the player's save data number.

It seems that originally there were going to be 7 days in the game, judging by graphics for Days 6 and 7, as well as an Exit button, being on the Day Select sprite sheet. It is unknown why the last 2 Days were scrapped.
