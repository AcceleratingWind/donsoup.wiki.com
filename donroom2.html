Cathedral Room (donroom2) is a room accessible from Day 4.

Features

The room features walls and the floor which are reminiscent of cathedrals and churches.

The object seems to be a mage of some kind, with glowing green eyes and yellow patterns on a shirt with bright green sleeves.
