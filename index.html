Welcome to the Soup 0.9 Wiki.

Soup 0.9 is a game described as a "looking around simulator", which was created by a team led by Yarhalla/Don.

It was originally released on April 10th, 2007, and to this day it remains mysterious and vague.

This website was created in hopes of helping others discover the game, and share insight on everything around it, as well as info on its mods and inspired games.
