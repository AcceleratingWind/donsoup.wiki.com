Mini Characters

Mini Characters are small moving objects that appear in all of the game's rooms. They don't make any sounds when they move and can't be interacted with.

They are always loaded with the room on a random distance from the room.

Variations

There are a total of 5 variations of them in the game:

• Mouse - Appears in most rooms, except for the ones mentioned below.

• Cockroach - Appears in the Town Room.

• Snail - Appears in the Rainy Room.

• Orb - Appears in the Flooded Rave Room.

• Invisible - "Appears" in the Dark Blue Room, it can't be seen since its graphic is transparent.
