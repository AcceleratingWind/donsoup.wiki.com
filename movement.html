Movement

Soup 0.9 has a pretty unique movement system, in terms of both player movement and camera movement.

Moving the camera is done with the arrow keys (and also the WASD keys as of version 0.91).

There is no limit to how far you can move the camera left or right, or even up and down, which allows the player to go upside down.

A "breathing" animation plays whenever the player isn't moving, and a small window image appears after some time of standing still, reminding the player of the game's controls.

To move, you have to hold shift and then use the keys mentioned above.

As you move, you can hear your footsteps which vary on the room you're in.
