Apartment Room (tarroom0) is a room accessible from Day 1. It was made by taro.

Features

The room looks like the outside view of an apartment complex, which encircles the area, with the floor having a grassy texture.

The object seems to be a child driving away on a bicycle, facing away from the camera in a similar fashion to Sky Room's object.
