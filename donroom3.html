Forest Room (donroom3) is a room accessible from Day 1. It was made by Yarhalla/Don.

Features

The room is seemingly located in the deep woods, with the music reflecting a sense of being lost in a forest with no sense of direction.

The object seems to be a faceless woman in dark clothes, and she can be seen holding a child.
