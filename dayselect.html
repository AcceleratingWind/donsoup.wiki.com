Day Select

Once you finish Day 5 of Soup 0.9, upon restarting the game and seeing the title screen you will be greeted by the Day Select. It is a pitch black screen with all of the Days being able to be revisited.

Day Select is unlocked once the number in the save data document reaches 5 and more, it can be disabled again by putting a number lower than 5 in it (negative numbers crash the game).

The Day Select replaces the Oyasuminasai Screen, though that can be reenabled by lowering the save data as said prior.

Interestingly, there appear to be unused graphics for Days 6 and 7, as well as an Exit button. Perhaps Days 6 and 7 were once planned to be playable, thus the game having a full week.

Music

In the song you can hear the Gabba Kick sound effect from the game LSD Dream Emulator. This is intentional, as the main leader of Soup 0.9 said in an interview.
