BOOPowo

BOOPowo is a mod for Soup 0.9, made by Fishy Pug Bruh. A prerelease build of it was published on the 23rd of June, 2021.

This mod is vastly incomplete, the latest build contains changed Day 1 rooms, as well as Day Select graphics, the Title Screen segment, the Oyasuminasai Screen and the transition from one room to another.

The parts of the mod that were changed focus on internet memes and shitposts. It is clear that its more of a joke mod rather than something serious.

NOTE: The transitions between days are broken and crash the game.
