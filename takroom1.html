Pyramid Room (takroom1) is a room accessible from Day 4. It was made by Akira Takagi.

Features

The room features grey and red gradient patterns on the walls and a cyan floor with a shadowy circle close to the center.

The object contrasts with the rest of the room, as it is a simple grey pyramid, with the light source seemingly coming from it's side
