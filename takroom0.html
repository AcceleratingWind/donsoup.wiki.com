Corrupted Drawing Room

Corrupted Drawing Room (takroom0) is a variation of Drawing Room, accessible from Day 5. It was made by Akira Takagi.

Features

The room and object are the same as Drawing Room's, except the skies are red, with black bleeding clouds.

The object has also changed, with in now being an inverted version of the original.

This rooms also shares the wind "song" with the Poem Room from the previous day.
