Rooms

In total, there are 25 rooms across 5 days that the player can visit and look around in. Each room appears randomly after the other.

After the player leaves the fifth room, the game either closes itself after showing the Oyasuminasai Screen, or sends the player back to the Day Select if the save data number is 5 or more.

Important: The names of the rooms aren't official. Next to them, in parentheses, are how they are listed inside of the game's files.
