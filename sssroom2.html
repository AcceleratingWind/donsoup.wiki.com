Sky Room (sssroom2) is a room accessible from Day 1. It was made by Sasa Sasaki.

Features

The room is seemingly up in the sky full of clouds, along with a piano playing in the background in front of some reversed noise.

The object seems to be a school girl facing away from the player, similar to Apartment Room's object.
