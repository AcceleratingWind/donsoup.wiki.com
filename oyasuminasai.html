Oyasuminasai Screen

Each time you finish a Day, you see a white screen with Japanese text that says "おやすみなさい".

"おやすみなさい" roughly translates from Japanese to "Good Night".

Some time after the screen appears, the game automatically closes itself, and when opened again, procedes with the next day up to Day 5.

After finishing Day 5 (or having the save data number equal 5 or more), this screen will no longer be shown after finishing a Day, as the Day Select will be unlocked and the game will send you back to the day selection instead.
