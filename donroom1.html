Octopus Room (donroom1) is a room accessible from Day 4. It was made by Yarhalla/Don.

Features

The room's walls feature rather large black holes in them, the floor is white with some sort of particles on it.

In the center of it all lies a faded pink-ish octopus-like creature, with the top of it's head being yellow-ish. It's looking to the left and seems to be smilling.
