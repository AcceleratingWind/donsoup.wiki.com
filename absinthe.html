absinthe

absinthe is a fangame of Soup 0.9, made by yoka in Unity. It was released on May 15th, 2021.

The fangame's rooms vary in content and the vibes they give off, but they are usually pretty tame compared to the original game.

Many rooms also seem to have more going on in them, having various other objects be in a single room, rather than simply being largely empty closed spaces with textures for the walls and the floor.

You don't have to hold shift to walk and the camera is controlled with the mouse. You touch the walls of the room you're in to go to a different (seemingly random) one, which happens pretty much instantly with no transition. There isn't a Day system, you simply go around various rooms.

At the moment, the game hasn't been updated since release. The creator has stated that they would like to make an update, but they have been too busy to start.
