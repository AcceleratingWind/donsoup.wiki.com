Prefixes

Internally, the names of the rooms use 3 letter prefixes before the word "room", symbolising the creator of the room. Afterwards they have a number next to them, presumably showing the chronology of the room's original creation.

Further more, they are also usually used in the names of the tracks, with exceptions being "ameasi", "bird", "choir" and "wind".

Said prefixes are listed in the "soup", the translated "soup.en" documents, as well as the "data" document in the "src" folder.

Below is a list of them (in the way they are listed in the "soup.en" document), with their rooms next to them.

Prefix List

• don - Don/Yarhalla (donroom1, donroom2, donroom3, donroom4, donroom5, donroom6)

• kat - Takahiro Kato (katroom0, katroom1)

• rua - ijjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjjj (ruaroom0, ruaroom2, ruaroom3)

• sss - Sasa Sasaki (sssroom0, sssroom1, sssroom2, sssroom3)

• sup - supply (suproom0)

• tak - Akira Takagi (takroom0, takroom1, takroom2)

• tar - taro (tarroom0, tarroom1, tarroom2, tarroom3, tarroom4)

• wan - Wang-zhi (wanroom0)
