Flooded Rave Room (katroom1) is a variation of Rave Room, accessible from Day 5. It was made by Takahiro Kato.

Features

The room seems to be raining, and the floor is flooded, evident by the footsteps heard when you walk on it.

The object here is the same as Rave Room's, however it is now fully white and seems to emit light, with the blue and red orbs now being black and white.
