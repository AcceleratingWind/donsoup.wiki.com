VineRooms

VineRooms is a mod for Soup 0.9, made by Agent-Red Jackal. It was released in April of 2018.

This mod centers around content and memes concieved by the channel Vinesauce. There is also some parts of it that look glitchy on purpose, which is also a reference to Vinesauce

As said prior, the game's Day Select graphics have been corrupted to resemble glitches in other video games, however the unused Days 6 and 7 graphics remain unchanged.
