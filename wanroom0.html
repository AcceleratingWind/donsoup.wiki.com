Cyber Room (wanroom0) is a room accessible from Day 2. It was made by Wang-zhi.

Features

The room has a futuristic aesthetic, with green and black neon colors and fast paced music.

The object is a variant of Ocean Room's object from the same day, having a greener tint and scan lines that fits the aesthetic of the room.
