Rave Room (katroom0) is a room accessible from Day 1. It was made by Takahiro Kato.

Features

The room is seemingly set in a night club setting, with the walls being that of a night starry sky, and the music being of a similar nature.

The object seems to be a white hand with nails painted red and blue, doing a "rock on" gesture, with two orbs floating on the two fingers.

The object in this room reappears later on, in the Flooded Rave Room.
